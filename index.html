About

The “WiseWingz” is an innovative solution developed during INKRIT 2.0 Hackathon Competition, addressing accessibility issues for hearing-impaired students, bridging gaps, and ensuring equal education opportunities. With today's technology, people are inclined to use an easier way to complete tasks.students also prefer e-learning. Because visual,aural & oral representation is an effective and fast paced method to convey information. But this is not applicable for students with hearing and speaking disabilities. .

  • Avatar taught video's


     Avatars to lead the user through their journey. For normal students avatar will be explaining the concepts orally, whereas for disabled users avatar will be explaining using sign language

  • Progress tracking

    Facilitate the educational journey for deaf and mute students through sophisticated progress tracking on our platform. Monitor sign language proficiency, achieve milestones, and cultivate a success-driven, accessible learning environment.

  • Easily understandable

    videos with captions

    Interactive videos that visualize the concepts and codes more detailed along with captions to support. Videos will be explaining concepts by highlighting the important points to be noted. Explanation to codes will also include the common errors that may occur and how to correct them. For needy users, sign language - interpretation videos are made available and can enable it on choosing the same

  • Users get to choose

    the type of video they want

    Not all the users need the sign language interpretation thus the user gets to choose the type of video they need - sign language interpretation video or normal video.
